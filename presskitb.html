ABOUT

Chore Boys are a New Orleans-based DJ duo bringing high-energy dance music to venues across the city. With a combined 100 years of experience in lifestyle improvement projects, they've become known for their eclectic sets that blend genres and keep dance floors moving.

From intimate club nights to massive dance parties, Chore Boys deliver curated experiences that showcase their deep knowledge of electronic music and commitment to creating unforgettable moments on the dance floor.

THE BOYS
PHOTO: m3ssy

m3ssy

Known for pushing boundaries and blending unexpected sounds, m3ssy brings an experimental edge to the Chore Boys sound. With roots in underground electronic music, his sets are characterized by bold track selections and seamless transitions that keep audiences guessing.

PHOTO: Rusty Lazer

Rusty Lazer

A veteran of New Orleans nightlife, Rusty Lazer brings decades of DJ experience and an encyclopedic knowledge of dance music. His technical precision and deep crate-digging skills complement m3ssy's experimental approach, creating the perfect balance in the Chore Boys sound.
