WHO THE BOYS ARE

Chore Boys is the newest project of DJs Messy and Rusty Lazer. They met on a video shoot for Bounce artist Big Freedia not long after Hurricane Katrina. Rusty was DJ’ing and managing Big Freedia and Nicky Da B and Casey was producing Beasts of the Southern Wild. In 2024 life conspired to deepen their friendship and they bonded over mutual support and music. They’re friends first, musicians second, and DJ’s most recently, performing and producing for a combined 31 years. Their sets are on-the-fly hand-crafted remixes and experiments sourced from every genre, era, decade, texture, fashion and emotion they’ve absorbed along the way.
