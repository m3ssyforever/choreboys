THE COLLABORATION

"Chore Boys X Ha Sizzle" is an adventure in cross-cultural collaboration and play.

Rusty Lazer (1/2 of live-remix duo Chore Boys) and New Orleans Bounce legend Ha Sizzle met years ago during Rusty's time managing, DJ'ing and touring globally with Big Freedia, Katey Red and Nicky Da B. In 2019 they traveled to Europe together for a series of festival and club shows around France and Belgium that deepened their friendship and creative bond.

The members of Chore Boys, Rusty and Messy, met in 2010 on a Big Freedia video shoot that Messy was producing (while also working on the Oscar nominated film Beasts of the Southern Wild). Their ties strengthened recently through shared musicianship (drums and guitar respectively) and the development of a rapid fire style of dance floor mayhem punctuated by elements of classic dance floor hits and obscure personal faves. They played several sets at Glastonbury in 2025 and Rusty has toured as a solo artist in Europe, the U.S. and Asia repeatedly since 2011.

A "Chore Boys X Ha Sizzle" performance features hours of dynamic improvised live-remixing across dozens of genres, styles and eras by Rusty and Messy, with Ha Sizzle on the mic hyping the crowd. No one can turn a group of strangers into friends and convert wallflowers into dancing machines like Ha Sizzle.

Every show also features Ha Sizzle performing a set of his own iconic work from 30 years in New Orleans Bounce Music, including collaborations and samples from projects with Drake, City Girls, Sexyy Red, Missy Elliot and many more.
